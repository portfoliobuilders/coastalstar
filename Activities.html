Health, Safety & Environment

At Coastal Star, we prioritize the health and safety of our workforce and clients across every project. Our HSE policies and procedures are designed to ensure the highest standards of workplace safety.

Our Health & Safety Commitments:

  • 100% workforce compliance with essential safety and regulatory requirements.
  • Third-party training for all HSE requirements, including work at height, harness training, and specific training for tools and equipment.
  • Regular implementation and monitoring of work environments based on risk assessments.

Every project begins with a comprehensive safety briefing and risk assessment. Our team is fully trained and equipped with proper PPE to ensure maximum protection on site.
