Who We Are

About Coastal Star

Coastal Star is a reputed manpower supply company based in Dubai, UAE, offering workforce and subcontracting solutions across sectors like civil, construction, mechanical, electrical, logistics, and hospitality. With over 500 trained professionals, we deliver high-quality labor across civil and MEP trades, serving leading developers and contractors.

Our Services

We specialize in skilled manpower supply, interior fit-out, subcontracting services, private recruitment, and temporary staffing. Our Interiors Division provides turnkey solutions for ceiling partitions, gypsum works, tiling, painting, joinery, and more.

Fully licensed under the Dubai Government, we ensure complete legal compliance while connecting the right talent with the right opportunities to support the region's growth.

Vision & Mission

Driving excellence in workforce solutions across the UAE

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect skilled, dependable, and experienced professionals with top-tier companies across the UAE. Coastal Star is committed to providing quality manpower, recruitment, temporary staffing, and subcontracting services that fulfill the unique needs of every client.

We strive to ensure complete satisfaction for both employers and employees by maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and service excellence.

As a trusted manpower partner in Dubai, we aim to deliver consistent value and build long-term, result-oriented relationships.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading manpower and subcontracting service provider in Dubai and the UAE, offering reliable workforce solutions and creating meaningful career opportunities.

We are dedicated to supporting the industrial growth of the UAE by supplying top-quality manpower in civil, construction, mechanical, electrical, plumbing, MEP, logistics, hospitality, and more.

At Coastal Star, we aim not only for business growth but also to contribute to the social and economic development of the nation while ensuring full compliance with the rules and regulations set by the Government of Dubai.

Our Journey

Company History

From our humble beginnings to becoming one of Dubai's trusted workforce solution providers

2010

Foundation

Coastal Star was established with a vision to provide quality manpower solutions to the growing construction sector in Dubai, starting with a team of just 25 professionals.

2014

Expansion

Expanded our services to include specialized trades and grew our workforce to over 200 professionals, serving major projects across Dubai.

2018

Diversification

Launched our Interiors Division to provide comprehensive fit-out solutions and added recruitment and temporary staffing services to our portfolio.

2023

Present Day

Today, Coastal Star stands as a trusted partner with over 500 skilled professionals, providing comprehensive workforce solutions to leading companies across the UAE.
